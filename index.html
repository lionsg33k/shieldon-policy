Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our OTP Manager app handles your information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All One-Time Password (OTP) secrets are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server or third-party service.

2. Local Storage

The app uses secure local storage (via AsyncStorage) to save your OTP entries. This data is stored on your device only and is not accessible by us or any third party.

3. Camera Permissions

We request access to your camera only when adding a new OTP via QR code. The camera is used solely for this purpose and never records or stores any photos or videos.

4. Security

Your data security is important. All stored OTP secrets remain on your device, and we recommend using secure PIN or biometric lock features to further protect access to the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be published on this page.

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, you may contact us at your@email.com.
